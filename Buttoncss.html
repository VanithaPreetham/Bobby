Instructions for online registration Steps for Online Registration 1. Click on “New Registration”.
2. Select the Vacancy No from the drop down list.
3. Select the Post Applied from the drop down list.
4. Enter your personal details.
5. Enter your date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format(Refer the advertisement for eligibility and relaxations if any).
6. Enter a valid e-mail id. Ensure that e-mail id is active throughout the entire selection process.
7. If applying for the post of GET, enter your GATE Registration No. and GATE Examination Paper.
8. Once you submit the registration form, a Registration No is generated. This Registration No. will serve as your Login ID.
9. The Registration No. and Password will be sent to your email id. Password is case sensitive.
Steps to fill Online Application Form
1. Click on “Applicant Login”
2. Enter your Login Id and Password
3. Enter Your Personal Details(All mandatory fields marked in "*")
4. Fill up the Educational qualification 5. While filling up your Professional Experience, ‘Date From’ and ‘Date To’ should be entered in DD/MM/YYYY format only. For ex: 01-Jan-2014 should be entered as 01/01/2014. Any other date format will be rejected.
6. All images must have the name without spaces.Please do not use any special characters anywhere in the application.
7. Upload your color photograph and signature(as per mentioned size and format).
8. Click on the declaration box prior submission of details.
9. Click on Submit button to save your details.
10. For completion of application go back to the Main page and complete the payment if required.
Steps for making Payment (If Applicable)
1. Ensure that you have a printer connected to your computer
2. Click on “Applicant login”
3. Click on “Make Payment”
4. The payment details shows the amount to be paid to the bank based on your category
5. Post successful payment candidates cannot change og modify the application details.Hence check the details before payment Steps for generating Application Form online
1. It may be noted that, application form will only be generated after making payment to the bank and updating details of payment.
2. Ensure that Pop-ups are unblocked in your browser before printing your application.
3. Click on “Print Registration Card” to print your application

It involves providing essential information, often personal details, to create a new account or become a member of a particular platform, system, or organization. New registrations commonly serve as the initial step toward accessing various benefits, privileges, or resources associated with the entity or service being joined.

"Applicant Login" pertains to the designated portal or interface where individuals seeking a position, admission, or participation in a program log in to access and manage their application details.
This secure digital platform enables applicants to submit required documents, track application progress, communicate with administrators or recruiters, and sometimes even complete assessments or interviews.
The applicant login serves as a personalized gateway for candidates, streamlining the application process and providing a centralized hub for managing their candidacy efficiently.
